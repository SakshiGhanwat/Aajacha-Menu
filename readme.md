🍱 Codies Mess

Effortless meal planning for a healthier you - with Codies Mess!

Codies Mess is a smart meal planning and mess management platform that helps users easily plan their daily meals while also enabling hotels and mess owners to grow their business digitally.

🌟 About the Project

Meal planning can often be stressful due to lack of time and ideas. Codies Mess solves this by:

Simplifying daily meal planning for users
Providing diverse and healthy meal options
Helping mess owners and hotels manage and expand their business
Reducing mealtime confusion with smart suggestions
🚀 Features
👨‍🍳 For Users:
🍽️ Easy meal planning system
📅 Daily/weekly meal suggestions
🥗 Variety of food options
❤️ Simple and user-friendly UI
🏨 For Mess/Hotel Owners:
📊 Business management dashboard
🧾 Menu updates and control
📈 Helps in business growth
👥 Reach more customers digitally
